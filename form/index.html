KAAL CHAKRA

As each grain of Time is destroyed, The next comes along and brings with it either Creation or Destruction. This is the Dance of Shiva.

Jyotisha | Occult | Numerology

At the end of Time, there is dissolution of the entire Creation and all living as well as non-living matter is absorbed unto itself to once again Begin at the Beginning.
